Purpose-built for well log digitizing

From legacy log image to usable subsurface data.

Focused tools help you extract log curves quickly while keeping calibration and final quality under your control.

Well log curve extraction

Extract colored or monochrome log curves using color and tolerance controls.

Flexible log image input

Open scanned logs, screenshots, and common raster image formats, or paste an image from the clipboard.

Visual quality control

Compare the source image and digitized result side by side, inspect points, and correct mismatches.

Color, grayscale, and fill workflows

Digitize colored curves, grayscale traces, and filled well-log intervals.

Depth and scale calibration

Map image coordinates to measured depth and curve values before digitizing.

LAS and CSV export

Export calibrated digital curves as LAS or CSV, with PNG output for visual review.

CurveTrace can also digitize other vertically oriented scientific curves and charts.

A clear workflow

Five steps from well log image to digital data.

  1. 01Import

    Open a scanned well log, screenshot, or raster log image.

  2. 02Select track

    Define the log track or region you want to digitize.

  3. 03Calibrate

    Set measured depth and curve scale references.

  4. 04Digitize & review

    Extract the curve, compare it with the source, and correct it visually.

  5. 05Export

    Save the calibrated result as LAS, CSV, or a review image.

Designed for real-world well log images

Keep control when legacy log images are less than perfect.

Adjust masks, color tolerance, fill direction, and curve points while comparing the result with the original log image. CurveTrace combines fast extraction with hands-on correction for practical well log QC.
